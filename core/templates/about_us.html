About Us

The Africa Startup Ecosystem Builders Society (ASEB Society) is a pan-African “network of networks” uniting ecosystem builders, entrepreneurs, policymakers, and organizations dedicated to advancing inclusive and resilient startup ecosystems across the continent. As the African counterpart to global ecosystem-building movements, ASEB Society serves as a collaborative platform to amplify local expertise, share resources, and co-create solutions to systemic challenges—empowering communities to build economies where innovation thrives for all.

Using a network-driven approach, we connect ecosystem builders, startups, investors, and institutions under a shared vision, enabling collective action to address barriers like funding gaps, policy fragmentation, and talent development. Together, we advance the principles of ecosystem building as a strategic model for economic transformation.

  • Our Mission

    To advancestartup ecosystem building as a strategic approach to economic development , fostering inclusive, sustainable, and thriving startup ecosystems across Africa.

  • Our Vision

    We envision a future where African economies are more resilient, equitable, and innovative, powered by strong and interconnected startup ecosystems
